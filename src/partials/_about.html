Een beetje over mij

I ’m Daan — a wedding photographer based in Belgium with a passion for capturing real emotions and unforgettable moments. I may be new to wedding photography, but I’ve quickly fallen in love with the energy, joy, and emotion that fill every wedding day. For me, photography is all about people — the laughter, the happy tears, the quiet looks that say everything. I don’t believe in stiff poses or forced smiles; I want my couples to feel relaxed, have fun, and just be themselves while I document it all. While Belgium is home, I’m excited to bring my camera (and curiosity) across Europe, telling love stories wherever they happen. Every wedding is unique, and I can’t wait to capture yours — with honesty, warmth, and a little bit of adventure.
